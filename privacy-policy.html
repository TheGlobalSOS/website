Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 10, 2025

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and contact information
  • Emergency contact details
  • Location data
  • Medical information (optional)
  • Travel itineraries and documents

1.2 Location Data

To provide emergency services and location-based features, we collect:

  • Real-time GPS location
  • Location history during emergencies
  • Embassy and emergency services proximity data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide emergency assistance
  • Connect you with relevant embassies and services
  • Manage your travel safety features
  • Send safety alerts and updates
  • Improve our services

3. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data, including:

  • End-to-end encryption
  • Secure data storage
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

4. Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Emergency services when required
  • Embassy services during emergencies
  • Your designated emergency contacts

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: contact@theglobalsos.com
